## What is Gradintel?

Gradintel is a web app that takes the guesswork out of your grades. Instead of wondering "what do I need on the final?", Gradintel tells you — down to the exact percentage — for every grade you're aiming for, across every course.

It syncs to the cloud, works on any device, and takes about 2 minutes to set up.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📈 GPA Tracking
- Real-time cumulative GPA across all semesters
- Supports 4.0, 4.3, and 5.0 scales
- Handles P/NP, Withdrawals, and grade replacement policies
- GPA trend chart across your full academic history

### 🧮 What Do I Need to Score?
The core feature. Enter your exam weights and current scores — Gradintel instantly tells you what you need on every remaining exam to hit any target grade.

- Scores calculated on  taken exams only  — untaken exams don't drag your grade down
- Per-exam breakdown with a safe range (e.g. "need 74% — safe between 71–77%")
- Instant feedback as you log new scores

### 🔮 What-If Simulator
Drag sliders to simulate different exam outcomes and see your projected GPA update live — without changing any real data.

Includes a  GPA Time Machine : see how your cumulative GPA would look if you'd scored differently across all past semesters.

### 🎯 Score Lab
-  Target Grade Calculator  — pick the grade you want, get the exact number you need
-  Safe Zone Indicator  — Green / Yellow / Red status per course
-  Smart Study Plan  — AI-ranked priority list of which courses need your attention most

### 🤖 AI Study Assistant
Ask anything in plain English:
- *"What should I focus on to get a B+ in Calculus?"*
- *"I have 3 exams left — what's my strategy?"*
- *"Give me a study plan for the next 2 weeks"*

Works out of the box with no API key. Optionally connect Groq (free), Gemini (free tier), Claude, or OpenAI for a faster or smarter experience.

### 📋 Canvas LMS Sync
Import your grades directly from Canvas — no extensions, no IT approval, works at any university running Canvas.

-  Bookmarklet method : drag once to your bookmarks bar, click once on Canvas — all courses sync instantly
-  CSV method : download from Canvas Grades, upload here — done in 30 seconds

### 📄 Syllabus Upload
Upload your syllabus PDF and AI extracts the course structure for you — exam names, weights, and credit hours auto-populated.

### 📅 Planner
- Pomodoro timer with course logging
- Deadline tracker with urgency warnings and Canvas import
- Course notes with auto-save
- Activity streak grid (GitHub-style)
- Mood log with trend chart

### 🏆 Achievements & XP
20+ unlockable badges and 10 XP levels — from Freshman to Valedictorian — earned by hitting academic milestones and staying consistent.

### 🎓 Degree Planner
Track your full degree requirements across multiple degrees. Upload your university's requirements PDF and AI fills in the course list automatically.

### 📊 Analytics
- Subject DNA radar chart across subject categories
- Semester-over-semester GPA delta
- Performance heatmap
- Grade distribution chart
- Danger Zone tracker vs your GPA goal

### 📤 Export
JSON backup, CSV spreadsheet, text report, and ICS calendar export for exam dates (Google / Apple / Outlook Calendar).

## 🔒 Privacy

- Your data lives in your own account — no one else can see it
- The Canvas bookmarklet uses your existing browser session — no Canvas credentials are ever stored
- AI API keys (if you add one) are stored locally in your browser only, never sent anywhere except the AI provider you choose
- No ads. No tracking. No third-party analytics
